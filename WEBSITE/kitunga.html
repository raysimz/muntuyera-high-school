About Our School

Muntuyera High School is a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and character development since its establishment.

Our History & Mission

Founded in 1985, Muntuyera High School has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and spiritually.

We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who will make meaningful contributions to society. Our values of integrity, excellence, and service guide everything we do.

How to Apply

Mutunyera High School admits students based on academic merit, character, and potential to contribute to our school community.

1

Complete Application Form

Fill out the online application or download the form from our website.

2

Submit Required Documents

Include academic records, birth certificate, and recommendation letters.

3

Entrance Examination

All applicants must sit for our entrance assessment.

4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with the admissions panel.
